Amelie

Amelie Zhang, a grade 12 student from Unionville High School who is fluent in English, French and Chinese. She is an active individual who takes initiative in everything she enjoys doing. She is currently the President of UHS Science Club, UHS French Club, Vice-President of UHS robotics club and team, and an initiator for STEM for Girls.

As a leader in UHS robotics club and team, she demonstrates initiative, collaboration and leadership skills by looking over 14 sections including building, web development, business and so on. With Amelie’s effort in outreach, UHS robotics club welcomed 130 more members and raised $17 000. In Science club and French club, she teaches various topics and labs that students are not able to learn or do in class.
Amelie started coding java in grade 6, she has self-learned Python, C++, Dart and XCode. She is currently working on a software app with her team to develop a more efficient way to help communicate between team members and alliance teams during First Robotics Competition(FRC) competition and VRC competition.
Aside from the interests of STEM learning, She enjoys playing the piano. She obtained her Licentiate Diploma in Piano Performance in 2018 and is a continuous winner in many local competitions such as Markham Music Festival and CCC Music Festival. To help the community, the joined Tri-M in school to perform piano every month at senior homes with other music students.
In April 2018, her friend and she initiated a charity and raised around $3000 to cancer patients and their families. Amelie’s dream is to become an engineer and help to create a better quality of life for people, especially the ones who are in need.
