Who We Are

Team 81208X, LeRoi Robotics, is a VRC team established by a couple of high school students from Markham, York Region.

The team consists of students from St. Robert Catholic High school, Unionville High School, Markville Secondary School and Pierre Elliott Trudeau High School.
The mission of LeRoi Robotic is to promote and further develop skills in science, technology, engineering, art and mathematics (STEAM) as well as computer science and business administration. To spark the magic of STEAM learning through our commitment to leadership, initiative, sharing and returning to the community, the team designs and builds an innovated robot to face the VEX EDR Change Up challenge.

As a team of hardworking, dedicated and passionate students, we wish to learn from other more experienced teams and to share our knowledge while giving back to the community.
