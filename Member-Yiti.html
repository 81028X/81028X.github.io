An undergraduate at the University of Waterloo, mentor of LeRoi robotics.

A past member of LeRoi Robotics, her interests often involve exploring unfamiliar topics. She have a wide range of hobbies including drawing, playing music, and improving her physical abilities through badminton and fencing. However, playing video games is the catalyst to her interest in programming, where both her passions and talents belong. In her spare time, she learned several programming languages such as C++, C, Java, JavaScript, HTML, and Visual Basic. Her curiosity gradually leads her pathway toward robotics and game development.
